Founding of CJ's Potato Chips

I founded CJ's Potato Chips in 2024 as the effects of inflation were causing a decrease in quality of snacks and an increase of price. I had noticed a large market for affordable, tasty potato chips and decided that I would try my hand at making them myself. This business started as just a small home business, selling my products at a local farmers market, but our products were more popular than we could have expected. Now we have, and still are quickly expanding, while still controlling our growth to avoid the high expenses other chip companies have that make a price similar to ours unachievable.

About Our Products

All products made at CJ's Potato Chips are made with a focus on quality. We have an ever expanding selection of flavors, which includes, original, barbeque, sour cream and onion, cheddar, and jalapeno.
